Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from just Googling financial advice?

Google gives you generic information. I give you a personalized plan based on YOUR income, YOUR debts, YOUR goals, and YOUR life situation. Plus, I hold you accountable and adjust the plan as your life changes. Information is easy to find. Implementation is hard. That's what I help with.

What if I've tried budgeting before and failed?

Most people fail at budgeting because they use the wrong system for their personality and life. I'll help you find the approach that actually works for you—not some one-size-fits-all spreadsheet you found online.

Are you a Certified Financial Planner (CFP)?

No, I'm not a CFP. I'm a financial coach with a degree in personal finance and years of experience helping people build wealth. I provide education, frameworks, and accountability—not specific investment recommendations like "buy this stock." If you need investment-specific advice, I'll help you find a licensed advisor.

What happens after the 90 days?

You'll have all the tools, knowledge, and systems to continue on your own. Many clients choose to check in quarterly for tune-ups (at a discounted alumni rate), but it's not required. My goal is to make you financially independent—not dependent on me.

What if I'm not sure this is right for me?

Book a free 20-minute consultation. We'll talk about your situation, and I'll tell you honestly if I think I can help. No pressure, no sales pitch—just a real conversation about your money.
