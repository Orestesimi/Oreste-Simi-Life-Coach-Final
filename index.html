Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from just Googling financial advice?

Google gives you generic information. I give you a personalized plan based on YOUR income, YOUR goals, and YOUR life situation. Plus, I teach you WHY we're making each decision so you gain the financial literacy to make great choices on your own. Information is easy to find. Implementation and true understanding? That's what I provide.

Are you a Certified Financial Planner (CFP)?

No, I'm not a CFP. I'm a financial coach with a degree in personal finance, business management experience at Caltech, and multiple side businesses. I provide education, frameworks, and accountability, not specific investment product recommendations like "buy this stock." If you need investment specific advice or products, I'll help you find a licensed advisor.

What if I've tried budgeting or financial planning before and failed?

Most people fail because they use the wrong system for their personality and life. I help you find the approach that actually works for YOU, not some one size fits all spreadsheet. Plus, I teach you the principles behind why certain strategies work, so you understand what you're doing and can stick with it.

Do you work with business owners?

Yes! The Executive Package specifically includes business financial planning: LLC vs S-Corp structuring, tax strategies, profit optimization, and owner compensation strategies. I run multiple side businesses myself, so I understand the unique challenges entrepreneurs face.

What happens after my package ends?

You'll have all the tools, knowledge, and systems to continue on your own. That's the goal: financial independence. Many clients choose quarterly or monthly check ins as alumni, but it's not required. You're equipped to manage your money confidently without me.

How do sessions work?

All sessions are virtual via Google Meet or Microsoft Teams. We meet weekly for 45 to 60 minutes. Between sessions, you have email/text access for quick questions (unlimited for Foundations and Executive packages).

Can I switch packages or add more sessions?

Absolutely. If you start with Starter and realize you need more, we can upgrade you to Foundations or Executive and credit what you've already paid. You're never locked in. We adjust based on your needs.

What if I'm not sure which package is right for me?

Book a free 20 minute consultation. We'll discuss your situation and I'll assess which package makes the most sense, or if coaching isn't the right fit for you at all. No pressure, just real conversation.
